Nick Galbreath was founder and boardmember of Science Sciences, held roles of CTO and COO, leading to an $825M exit in 2022. Previously, Nick lead early-stage engineering, operations, and security groups ending with an astounding record of two IPOs and six major aquisitions. Nick often spoke on the intersection of engineering and security. In addition, Nick authored many patents, a cryptography, and a research paper or two. Nick has a MS in mathematics from Boston University, and BS from University of Minnesota, with mathematical and computer sciences research at Los Alamos and Argonne National Laboratories. He lives in Las Vegas, with his wife and two children.
